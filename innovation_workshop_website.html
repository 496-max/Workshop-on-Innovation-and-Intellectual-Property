About the Workshop

The Sultanate of Oman is placing growing emphasis on innovation and intellectual property, recognizing their pivotal role in driving economic growth, fostering creativity, and strengthening competitiveness.

In light of rapid technological advancements and global knowledge exchange, the need for structured, collaborative, and institutional approaches has become increasingly essential.

This intensive two-day program brings together diverse healthcare stakeholders including clinicians, administrators, and technology professionals to collaboratively address critical challenges within the healthcare and academic sectors.

The workshop employs evidence-based methodologies, combining theoretical frameworks with practical application through interactive sessions, design thinking sprints, and prototype development.
